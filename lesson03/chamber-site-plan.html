Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Elizabethtown Chamber of Commerce site is to promote the city and businesses of Elizabethtown, KY with the goal of increasing the prosperity of the community.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A business owner in Elizabethtown, KY wants to become a member of the chamber of commerce.

Scenario 2: A business owner from another city is considering expanding to Elizabethtown, KY and wants to get information about the community.

Scenario 3: A resident of Elizabethtown, KY wants to find out what local events are planned within the next month.
